A little bit about myeself

Eagle Rock - Topanaga, CA

Hailing from the East Coast in Central Ohio, i've made my way to Los Angeles in pursuit of a proffessional career. I have always had a deep interest and passion for all things tech. Eventually, this would grow into an obsession, and by my senior year in high school I had gotten an IT position with my school. This was the beginning of something amazing, and from there on I knew what I wanted to do with my time. This passion extends beyond simply IT, however.

Some interests of mine include guitar, programming, skateboarding, graphic design, video game design, and much more. If I can break it apart and see how it works, I find it interesting. This passion is what mainly brought me to LA. I hope to acheieve greater heights here and expand my working knowledge of not only the culture, but the industry as a whole.
